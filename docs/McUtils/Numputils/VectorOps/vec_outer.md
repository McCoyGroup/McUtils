```python
vec_outer(a, b, axes=None, order=2): 
```
Provides the outer product of a and b in a vectorized way.
We have to prioritize what goes where, and `order` determines
if the axes of `a` come first or `b` come first
  - `a`: `Any`
    > 
  - `b`: `Any`
    > 
  - `axis`: `Any`
    > 
  - `:returns`: `_`
    >
