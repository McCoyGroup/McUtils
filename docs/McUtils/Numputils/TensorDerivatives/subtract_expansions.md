```python
subtract_expansions(a_expansion, b_expansion, order=None): 
```
**LLM Docstring**

Subtract one expansion from another term by term, zero-padding to the requested
order.
  - `a_expansion`: `list`
    > the expansion to subtract from
  - `b_expansion`: `list`
    > the expansion to subtract
  - `order`: `int | None`
    > the order to pad to (inferred from the inputs if omitted)
  - `:returns`: `list`
    > the difference expansion
