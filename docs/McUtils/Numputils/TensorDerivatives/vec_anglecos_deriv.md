```python
vec_anglecos_deriv(A_expansion, B_expansion, order, unitized=False): 
```
**LLM Docstring**

Compute the derivative expansion of the cosine of the angle between two
vectors (the dot product of their unit vectors).

Unless the inputs are already unitized, each vector is first replaced by its
unit-vector expansion.
  - `A_expansion`: `list`
    > expansion of the first vector
  - `B_expansion`: `list`
    > expansion of the second vector
  - `order`: `int | list[int]`
    > the derivative order(s)
  - `unitized`: `bool`
    > whether the inputs are already unit vectors
  - `:returns`: `list`
    > the cosine expansion
