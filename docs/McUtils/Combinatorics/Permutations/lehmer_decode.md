```python
lehmer_decode(ndim, codes, dtype=None): 
```
**LLM Docstring**

Decode Lehmer-code integers back into their permutations (the inverse of
`lehmer_encode`).
  - `ndim`: `int`
    > the permutation length
  - `codes`: `np.ndarray`
    > the Lehmer codes
  - `dtype`: `Any`
    > the output dtype
  - `:returns`: `np.ndarray`
    > the permutations, shape `(..., ndim)`
