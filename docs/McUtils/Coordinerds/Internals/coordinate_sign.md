```python
coordinate_sign(old, new, canonicalize=True): 
```
**LLM Docstring**

Determine whether two equivalent coordinate specifications have the same or opposite orientation, returning `1`, `-1`, or `0` when they are not equivalent.
  - `old`: `Any`
    > Original coordinate orientation used as the sign reference.
  - `new`: `Any`
    > Coordinates newly added to the graph.
  - `canonicalize`: `Any`
    > Whether to put coordinates in canonical orientation before comparison or storage.
  - `:returns`: `int`
    > `1` for equal orientation, `-1` for reversed orientation, or `0` for nonequivalent coordinates.
